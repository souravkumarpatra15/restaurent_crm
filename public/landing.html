FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot

Do my customers need to download an app?
No. The QR menu opens in their browser — any phone, any browser. No app, no login, no friction.
Can I use my existing thermal printer?
Yes — DinoviX works with any ESC/POS-compatible 80mm or 57mm thermal printer connected over your WiFi network (TCP/IP). No drivers needed.
What happens when my internet goes down?
The POS and kitchen display continue to work as long as your local WiFi is up. Orders already in the system remain accessible. Cloud sync resumes when connectivity returns.
Is GST billing supported?
Yes. DinoviX supports CGST/SGST/IGST, multiple tax slabs per item, tax-inclusive and tax-exclusive pricing. Bills and reports are GST-compliant.
How many restaurants can I manage?
Starter supports 1 branch, Growth supports 3 branches, and Enterprise is unlimited. Each branch can have its own menu, staff, tables, and reports — all managed from one admin account.
Can I try it before paying?
Yes — every plan includes a 30-day free trial. No credit card required to start. If you decide DinoviX isn't for you, just don't renew.
Do you offer support in Hindi?
Yes — our support team is available in English and Hindi via WhatsApp, email, and phone during business hours (9 AM – 8 PM IST).
